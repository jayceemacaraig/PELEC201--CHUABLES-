Holiday Specials at Chuables: A Taste of the Seasons

The holiday season is the sweetest time of year, and at Chuables, we’re baking up something special just for you! Our limited-edition holiday treats capture the flavors and warmth of the season, perfect for sharing with loved ones or treating yourself.

  1. Spiced Gingerbread Mini Cake

    Nothing says the holidays like the comforting taste of gingerbread. Our Spiced Gingerbread Mini Cake is packed with warm spices and topped with a rich cream cheese frosting — perfect for festive gatherings.

  2. Peppermint Chocolate Dream

    For a holiday twist on a classic favorite, our Peppermint Chocolate Dream combines rich chocolate cake with a hint of refreshing peppermint, topped with crushed candy canes for that extra festive touch.

  3. Eggnog Delight Cake

    Indulge in the flavors of the season with our Eggnog Delight Cake. This moist, creamy cake is infused with classic eggnog spices, making it the perfect holiday treat.

  4. Winter Wonderland Dessert Box

    Our Winter Wonderland Dessert Box is filled with a variety of holiday-themed mini cakes, cookies, and brownies. It’s the ultimate gift for family, friends, or a festive dessert table centerpiece.

  5. Cinnamon Pecan Mini Cake

    Warm up with our Cinnamon Pecan Mini Cake, a delightful combination of cinnamon-spiced cake and crunchy pecans, topped with a buttery glaze. It’s cozy, comforting, and full of holiday cheer.

Celebrate the season with Chuables’ festive holiday specials. Whether you’re looking for the perfect gift or a sweet addition to your holiday feast, our seasonal treats will make your celebrations even sweeter. Available for a limited time — so don’t miss out!
